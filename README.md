A free web collection of music from around the world.

## Composition
This collection is a blend of two music collections from the different time periods.

### Music from the period 2019-present
This music collection contains songs that are discovered on the period from the late 2019 to present (median year 2020). Added NEW artists, previously unmentioned[^1], factually unmentioned[^2] or unheard of[^3], related artist[^4] or not: 2 Chainz (factually unmentioned in Ariana Grande - Rule The World song), De Hofnar, James Foxx, Kodak Black (previously unmentioned in T-PAIN - Florida Boy song), Lost Years, Mr. Talkbox (factually unmentioned in Bruno Mars - 24K Magic song), Tones and I, Travis Scott (factually mentioned in Travis Scott - Pick Up The Phone song), Victoria Justice (related artist: Ariana Grande, where related: Victorious cast), YoungBoy Never Broke Again, etc.
[^1]: Included in the song from the previous package of same domain, but not mentioned.
[^2]: Included in the song from the package of another content domain or same context, but not mentioned.
[^3]: Leading singer that was NEWLY added, missing in previous package of same domain.
[^4]: Not being mentioned anywhere in the song in which the related artist took part.
